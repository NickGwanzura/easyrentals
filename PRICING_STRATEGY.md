## 💳 Payment Structure

### Monthly vs Annual
- **Monthly**: Full price, cancel anytime
- **Annual**: 20% discount (2 months free)
- **Bi-annual**: 15% discount

### Payment Methods
- Credit/Debit cards
- Bank transfer (Enterprise)
- PayPal
- Stripe (automatic recurring)

### Billing Cycles
- Subscription renews on 1st of month
- Prorated upgrades/downgrades
- 7-day grace period for failed payments
